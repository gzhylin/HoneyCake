Placing and order

At this moment we are only accepting orders via phone. Call/Text 815-756-0906 Oksana.

Payment

We accept cash, Zelle or Venmo payments. We do not accept checks.

Pickup

We are located in Leander, TX. About 30 min drive from downtown Austin, TX
Pickup times are flexible.

Delivery

We offer delivery services, fee starts at 2$/mile with a minimum charge of 25$.
Delivery service need to be arranged at least 5 days before delivery date.

Shelf Life

All our cakes and pastry are prepared from scratch within 24 hours before pickup date.
Since we are using only high quality ingredients and no preservatives, all cakes and pastry must be consumed
within 72 hours after the pickup. Must be refrigerated 30 min after pickup.

Large Events

We offer service for large events like weddings, celebrations, spacial occasions.
It would be possible to order custom cakes, baked goods, eatable decorations, etc.

Sugar Free Cakes

At the moment we do not offer sugar free or gluten free pastry.
